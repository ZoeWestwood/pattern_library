Buttons

Buttons are always used within websites they are a simple element of interaction that are the gateway to a user using your site, buttons can be both simple and complicated.


Button 1 - Outline to Fill

Get the Code:

<button type="button" class="button1Purple">Purple</button>
<button type="button" class="button1Blue">Blue</button>
<button type="button" class="button1Red">Red</button>
<button type="button" class="button1Green">Green</button>
