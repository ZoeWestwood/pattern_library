Hero Image

A Hero Image is a static image that often fills the full width of a page. They regularly appear underneath or before a navigation bar and typically they will re-size accordingly depending on the size of the browser.

Example:



Get the Code:

<div class="banner">
	<img class="banner" src="images/computer.png" alt="Hero Image">
</div>
